What are the chat commands?

  • /name [name] - Change your name.
  • /speed [speed] - Change your speed.
    • Max value is 225, min value is 125.
  • /pitch [pitch] - Change your pitch.
    • Max value is 75, min value is 25.
  • /color [color] - Change your color. The ones available are:
    • red
    • brown
    • green
    • blue
    • purple
    • black
  • /youtube [video ID] - Play a YouTube video.
  • /asshole [name] - Call someone an asshole.
